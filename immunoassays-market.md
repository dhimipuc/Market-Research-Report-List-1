<p><strong>The Immunoassays Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Enzyme Immunoassay</li><li>Fluorescence Immunoassay</li><li>Chemiluminescence Immunoassay</li><li>Radioimmunoassay</li><li>Nephelometric Immunoassay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The immunoassay market comprises several key types, each utilizing specific detection methods. Enzyme Immunoassays (EIAs) employ enzymes to produce measurable signals. Fluorescence Immunoassays (FIAs) use fluorescent tags for sensitivity. Chemiluminescence Immunoassays (CLIAs) generate light signals for quantification. Radioimmunoassays (RIAs) leverage radioactive isotopes for detection, while Nephelometric Immunoassays assess light scattering to determine concentration. Each type targets diverse applications in diagnostics, enhancing accuracy and reliability in detecting biomarkers in various samples.</p></p>
