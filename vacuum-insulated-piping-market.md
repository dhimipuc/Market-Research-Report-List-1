<p><p>Vacuum Insulated Piping (VIP) is a cutting-edge technology designed to significantly reduce heat transfer between the contents of a pipeline and the external environment. The demand for VIP is driven by several sectors, including the oil and gas industry, industrial refrigeration, and medical applications where efficient thermal management is critical. </p><p>The Vacuum Insulated Piping Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 8.5% during the forecast period, fueled by advancements in technology and increased awareness of energy-efficient solutions.
