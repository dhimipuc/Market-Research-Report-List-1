<p><p>The amplifying stethoscopes market is primarily categorized into single-head and dual-head stethoscopes. Single-head stethoscopes feature a diaphragm that captures a wide range of frequencies, making them suitable for basic assessments. In contrast, dual-head stethoscopes have both a diaphragm and a bell, allowing for versatility in auscultation by picking up high- and low-frequency sounds. Both types cater to varying needs in healthcare settings, enhancing acoustic clarity and improving diagnostic accuracy for medical professionals.</p></p>
