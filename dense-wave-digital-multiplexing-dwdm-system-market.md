Within the DWDM market, Synchronous Optical Network Data (SONET) provides standardized, reliable data transfer for telecommunications. Internet Protocol (IP) facilitates internet data communications, ensuring efficient routing and transmission. Asynchronous Transfer Mode (ATM) offers a cell-based switching method for data, video, and voice, optimizing bandwidth and minimizing latency in diverse networking applications.</p></p>
